Methodology Note

This environmental assessment combines theoretical frameworks from higher education research with practical observations and institutional data to provide a comprehensive analysis of Union Street Center's residential environment. The assessment employs both qualitative and quantitative approaches to understand how physical and organizational factors influence student development and community engagement.
