Welcome to My Environmental Observation Assessment

Hello, and Welcome to my Environmental Observation Assessment of the Residence Life Department at Union Street Center, presented through the perspective of my role as an Assistant Coordinator for Center Management.

This project explores how environmental factors - both physical and organizational - shape the culture, operations, and engagement within the department. It also reflects on how my position contributes to creating living-learning environments at Union Street Center that support holistic student development, academic success, and civic engagement.

Use the navigation menu to learn more about Union Street Center – Residence Life, my professional role, and the observations and analyses that inform this assessment.

Assessment Overview

Physical Environment

An analysis of the physical spaces within Union Street Center, examining how architectural design and spatial organization impact student life and community development.

Layout & Function

Exploring the functional design of key spaces including the Center Desk, office areas, lounge spaces, library, and market, and their role in daily operations.

Impact of Physical Space

Understanding how the physical environment influences student behavior, social interaction, and academic engagement within the residential community.

Structural Environment

Examining the organizational structure, policies, and administrative systems that shape the residential life experience at Union Street Center.

CECE Indicators

Analyzing Cultural Engagement, Community Engagement, and Educational indicators that demonstrate the effectiveness of the residential environment.

Person-Environment Interaction

Investigating how individual students interact with and are influenced by the physical and social environment of Union Street Center.
